Apevia-CRUSADER-F-PK-ATX-Mid-Tower

At first glance, the Apevia Crusader flaunts an aggressive style particularly outstanding to the eyes. To satisify your needs, we included brilliant glowing LED RGB fans to simulate a light show perfect for your PC room along with a smoked tempered glass on the left side panel to showcase your accomplished build. Lastly, the distinct mesh design on the front panel provides additional air flow to your PC components while offering an impressive sight of the three RGB fans in the front. The Crusader case is ideal for all users; great for gamers or for those who want an attractive PC case.

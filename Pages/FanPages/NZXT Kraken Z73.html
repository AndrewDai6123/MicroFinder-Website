NZXT Kraken X53

The all-new Kraken Z series allows for new customizations of the all-in-one liquid cooling system. Thanks to the exclusive CAM software interface, it is possible to make fine adjustments with extreme ease; now it is possible to view animated gifs or information about the CAM system, for total customization. The Kraken Z series of liquid cooling systems, which come with a 6-year warranty, are characterized by superior performance, simple installation and a totally customizable look.

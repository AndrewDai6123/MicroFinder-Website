Cooler Master Hyper 212 Black Edition

The legendary Hyper air cooler is back and better than before. The Hyper 212 Black Edition offers better installation and great performance, easily one of the best air cooling solutions. The aluminum top cover and nickel plated jet black fins give the Hyper 212 Black Edition a more premium exterior appeal . The sleek jet black not only makes it look great, but also increase cooling performance.

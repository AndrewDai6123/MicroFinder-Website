Asus-ROG-STRIX-Z490-E-Gaming-ATX-LGA1200

The ASUS ROG Strix Z490-E (WiFi 6) Gaming motherboard is designed to cope with the demands of 10th Generation Intel® Core™ processors, with boosted power delivery and an optimized cooling design providing more surface area for heat dissipation. ROG Strix Z490-E Gaming features AI enhancements and intuitive dashboards to help you overclock and tweak various parameters easily.
